Privacy Policy

SIT Notes Mobile Application

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

Silicon Institute of Technology ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SIT Notes mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

By registering for an account or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

When you register for an account, log in, or make a purchase, we collect certain personal information that can be used to identify you, including:

  • Account Credentials: Name, email address, password stored securely using Firebase Authentication and your profile document.
  • Profile Preferences: Academic department and semester selection.
  • Transaction Details: Purchase history, transaction IDs, and payment status. We do not store full credit card numbers or payment credentials on our servers. These are handled securely by integrated payment service providers.

B. App Usage and Device Data

We may automatically collect technical and usage information when you use the App, such as:

  • Offline Downloads Information: Titles and paths of files downloaded locally to your device cache.
  • AI Assistant Queries: Input questions sent to the AI Study Assistant, used strictly to generate answers for your sessions.
  • Device Details: Operating system version, unique device identifiers, and generic performance logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: Including managing your student profile, syncing your preferences, and validating purchases.
  • To Process Transactions: To register payment records and unlock premium study notes or video courses.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To recommend study notes, PYQs, and courses relevant to your selected branch and semester.
  • To Improve the App: Monitoring performance, resolving crashes, and refining the AI Study Assistant search relevance.
  • To Secure the Service: Detecting and preventing unauthorized logins, spam, or malicious abuse of the Firestore database.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party cloud services to store your data and operate the App:

  • Firebase Services - Google LLC:
    • Firebase Authentication: Handles secure user registration, password hashing, and logins.
    • Cloud Firestore: Stores student profile documents, mapped branches, semesters, subjects, notes metadata, and purchase logs.
    • Firebase Storage: Hosts study notes cover thumbnails and assets.
  • Google Drive / Google Docs: Hosts and serves the PDF preview pages within our built-in PDF viewer.
  • Payment Gateways: Handles external secure transactions for unlocking paid content.

Your data is transferred and stored securely in accordance with Firebase's data protection agreements.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • We retain your personal data, including name, email, profile preferences, and purchases, for as long as your student account is active.
  • Data Deletion Rights: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data from our systems at any time. To request deletion, please contact our administrator at suyashsingh.mit@gmail.com. Upon request, all Firestore profile documents and Firebase Authentication records linked to your UID will be permanently removed.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We secure data transmission using HTTPS and enforce strict database-level authorization rules, including Firestore Security Rules, so that only you and administrators can access your personal profiles and purchase histories. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for students pursuing higher education at the university level. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
